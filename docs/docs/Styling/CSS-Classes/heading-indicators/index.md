---
title: Heading indicators
icon: material/format-letter-case
---

Add a heading indicator for all headings. Will appear in reading, editing and source mode.

Usage:
```md
cssclasses:
    - headings-indicator-all
---

# I will have an indicator before me
## I will have an indicator before me
### I will have an indicator before me
#### I will have an indicator before me
##### I will have an indicator before me
###### I will have an indicator before me
```
